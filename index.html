    Junction League's Nikon X1#

    Capabilities#

    • Spinning disk confocal unit optimized for high resolution imaging
    • Four lasers (405nm, 488nm, 561nm, 640nm)
    • Live-cell imaging (5% CO2 and temperature control)
    • Dual cameras for simultaneous two-channel confocal imaging
    • Inverted Nikon Ti2-E with Perfect Focus System for eliminating focus drift
    • Motorized stage and z-piezo stage allow for tiling/stitching and fast z-stacks
    • Selective photoactivation/photobleaching with a 405nm laser
